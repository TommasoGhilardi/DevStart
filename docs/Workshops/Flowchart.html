In this tutorial we will use a lot of different software and libraries. It is always complex to have all of them working for everyone. For this reason we created this simple flowchart to help guide you through the installation process!

Please realize that we are doing our best to have everyone follow the workshop on their machine, but this may not be feasible for some participants.

This visual guide will help you navigate through the installation process based on your specific needs and system configuration. Please use this flowchart and the links provided in it to proceed step by step.
